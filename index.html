    How Work

    UX design (user experience design) is a digital product design methodology to solve a human problem. This human-centred design approach ensures design teams make decisions based on users’ needs rather than assumptions.

    Key considerations include:

    • Who is the product for?
    • What does the audience expect or look for?
    • Is the product designed to inform, sell, or entertain?
    • How can it stand out from competitors?
    • What problem is this product solving?

    Understand

    Before you get started with any project, you need to get the basics down first. That means understanding two crucial elements.

    Research

    Interviews – Target audience and ask pointed questions about their issues. User/Focus groups, Surveys, Usability testing.

    Analysis

    User journey maps – A user journey map is a representation of the user’s interactions with your product.

    Design

    Design enhances user interaction and navigation through structure, aesthetics, typography, and visual hierarchy.

    Prototype

    A prototype is a functional model for testing and refining a product before development.

    Analyze (again)

    How are our users responding to the product? Did it solve their issues and pain points?
